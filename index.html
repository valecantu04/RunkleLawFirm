Why Choose Us

Due to recent legislation, access to the courts has been limited for some cases. Ryan Runkle has had some success in overcoming these limitations.

Steps to be Tested

1) We will evaluate any worker who belives he or she was exposed to Asbestos. You will pay no money for medical tests or legal fees unless you recieve compensation for your Asbestos exposure.

2) If you have significant occupational exposure, we will schedule an x-ray for you in your community and have that x-ray evaluated by a NIOSH Certified expert B-Reader.

3) If you have 'markings' of Asbestos exposure, we will schedule an examination with a Board Certified physician in your community.

4) If you have cancer, we will coordinate the review of your pathology materials by a Board Certified Pathologist.
